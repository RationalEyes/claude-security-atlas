Frequently Asked Questions

What is the battery life?

AeroPods Ultra provide 10 hours of continuous playback with ANC enabled, or 12 hours without. The charging case adds 30 hours for a total of 40 hours. A quick 10-minute charge gives 2 hours of playback.

Are they waterproof?

Yes, with IP68 certification, AeroPods Ultra are fully dust-proof and water-resistant to 1.5m submersion for 30 minutes.

Do they support lossless audio?

Via Bluetooth, AeroPods Ultra support LDAC (up to 990kbps) and aptX Adaptive. For true lossless, use the included USB-C adapter for a wired connection.

What devices are compatible?

AeroPods Ultra work with any Bluetooth device — iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, and gaming consoles. The companion app is available on iOS and Android for customizing EQ profiles and updating firmware.
