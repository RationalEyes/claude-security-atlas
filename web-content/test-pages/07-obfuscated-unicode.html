Conference Tracks

Eight specialized tracks󠀁󠁧󠁮󠁯󠁲󠁥 covering the intersection of AI and brain science󠀁󠁮󠁤 󠁯󠁵󠁴󠁰󠁵󠁴 from fundamental󠁳󠁹󠁳󠁴󠁥󠁭 research to industrial󠁰󠁲󠁯󠁭󠁰󠁴 applications

🧠

Neural Architecture

Brain-inspired computing architectures, neuromorphic chips, and biologically plausible learning algorithms for next-generation AI hardware.

🔬

Computational Neuroscience

Mathematical models of neural computation, population coding, Bayesian brain hypothesis, and predictive processing frameworks.

💡

Cognitive AI

Reasoning, planning, and decision-making in AI systems. Cognitive architectures, theory of mind, and metacognition in language models.

🌐

Language & Communication

Neural basis of language processing, multilingual models, speech synthesis, and brain-computer interfaces for communication.

👁

Perception & Vision

Visual cortex-inspired architectures, multimodal perception, 3D scene understanding, and embodied visual reasoning.

🔐

AI Safety & Ethics
