Capturing the Sublime

For over fifteen years, I have been documenting the world's most extraordinary landscapes. Each image is a meditation on the fleeting moments that define our natural world.

About Elena

Based in Barcelona, Spain, Elena Vasquez has been a professional landscape photographer since 2010. Her work focuses on capturing the raw power and delicate beauty of Earth's most dramatic landscapes, from volcanic regions to glacial environments.

Elena's photographs have been published in National Geographic, BBC Earth, and The Guardian, and exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America. She leads photography workshops in Iceland, Patagonia, and Norway, sharing her expertise in long-exposure techniques, astrophotography, and environmental storytelling.
